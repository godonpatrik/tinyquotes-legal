Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

Tiny Quotes (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a mobile app that lets parents record memorable quotes and moments from their children. We take your privacy seriously and collect only what the App needs to function. This policy explains what we collect, why, who processes it, and how you can delete it.

The App contains no advertising, no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

1. Who is responsible for your data

The data controller is the developer of Tiny Quotes. For any privacy question or request, contact us at support@tinyquotes.app.

2. What we collect

DataSourceWhy
Email address Provided by Apple when you sign in with Apple. This may be Apple’s private relay address if you choose to hide your email. To create and identify your account.
Name Optionally provided by Apple at first sign-in, or a display name you enter. To personalise the App.
User identifiers A Supabase account ID and the Apple user identifier. To link your account to your data and your subscription.
Your content Entered by you: children’s names, optional birthdates and colours, quotes/sayings, dates, and optional context notes and tags. This is the core purpose of the App — storing and showing your content back to you.
Purchase history Subscription status from Apple, via RevenueCat. To unlock and maintain Tiny Quotes Pro features.

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or device sensor data.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide the App’s core features (storing and displaying your children’s quotes).
  • To authenticate you and keep you signed in securely.
  • To manage your Tiny Quotes Pro subscription and entitlement.
  • To schedule local notifications on your device (daily quote reminders and anniversary reminders). These are generated on your device; we do not send push notifications from a server.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or profiling.

4. Service providers (processors)

We share data only with the providers required to run the App:

ProviderPurposeData
Supabase Database and authentication hosting. Servers are located in the EU (Ireland, eu-west-1). Account, identifiers, and your content.
RevenueCat Subscription management. Account identifier and subscription/purchase status.
Apple Sign in with Apple and App Store payments. Authentication and purchase processing per Apple’s own privacy policy.

5. Where your data is stored

Your account and content are stored in Supabase data centres in the European Union (Ireland). Access is protected by row-level security so that you can only ever read or write your own data.

6. How we protect your data

  • All network traffic uses encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Database access is restricted per-user with row-level security.
  • Your sign-in session is stored in the device’s secure keychain.
  • The Apple token used for account deletion is encrypted at rest.

7. Data retention & deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete account inside the App. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, children, and quotes, and revokes the App’s Sign in with Apple connection. Subscription event logs used for billing and support are automatically removed within 180 days.

8. Children’s privacy

Tiny Quotes is intended for use by parents and other adults. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly allow children to create accounts. Any information about a child in the App is content that you, the adult account holder, choose to enter and control.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. You can exercise most of these directly in the App, or by contacting support@tinyquotes.app. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be communicated in the App where appropriate.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@tinyquotes.app.
