Frequently asked questions

How do I sign in?

Tiny Quotes uses Sign in with Apple only — there is no separate password to remember. Tap “Sign in with Apple” on the welcome screen to create or access your account.

I already paid — how do I restore my subscription?

On the subscription screen in the App, tap Restore Purchases. As long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID you originally subscribed with, your access will be restored.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple. On your iPhone, go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, select Tiny Quotes, and change or cancel it there. Cancelling keeps your access until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the App, go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, your children, and all saved quotes, and revokes the App’s Sign in with Apple connection. This cannot be undone.

How do reminders and notifications work?

Tiny Quotes can send a gentle morning reminder with a random quote from your collection, plus reminders for your children’s special dates. You can turn these on or off in Settings. All reminders are scheduled locally on your device — we do not send push notifications from a server.

Which devices are supported?

Tiny Quotes is designed for iPhone running a recent version of iOS.
